The Great Meditation organized by Chicago U Sociology

Posted on 2021-05-07 In Updates Disqus:

Over the past two days, I have participated the Great Mediatation seminar organized by Chicago University Department of Sociology. Very inspiring panel discussions! Prof. Chris Bail also gave an very informative keynote speech about his recent research and the his new book of Breaking the Social Media Prism.

Transcripts and details could be found in the official website below: https://sociology.uchicago.edu/content/great-mediation

data resources for studying social media released by platforms themselves

Posted on 2021-05-07 In Resources Disqus:

Twitter

  1. Twitter releases data archives of state linked information operations constantly. https://transparency.twitter.com/en/reports/information-operations.html
  2. Twitter also regularly release data of information requests they received from governments, law enforcement, and third parties around the world. https://transparency.twitter.com/en/reports/information-requests.html#2020-jan-jun

Google

Will be attending SICSS Chicago

Posted on 2021-04-28 In Updates Disqus:

I will be attending the SICSS Chicago this summer rom Monday, June 14 to Friday, June 25, 2021.

The Summer Institute of Computational Social Science is organzied by the CSS commnity each summer and will usually be held in different cities and countries. This year, due to the pandemic, all session are virtual. Reading and coding materials are also avaiable online.
